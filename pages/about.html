Carbo Valencia Realty was born in Cavite — and we're proud of it. Since 2015, we've helped hundreds of Filipino families find a place they truly call home.

Who we are

A family business
rooted in Cavite

CVR — short for Carbo Valencia Realty — started as a small brokerage in Dasmariñas with one mission: make real estate straightforward and honest for every Filipino family looking for a home in Cavite.

We know these streets. We know which subdivisions flood in July, which barangays are walkable to public markets, and which developments actually deliver on their promises. That local knowledge is something no big broker from Manila can replicate.

Today our team of licensed brokers and accredited salespersons handles listings across Dasmariñas, Bacoor, Imus, General Trias, and the wider CALABARZON region — always with the same personal touch we started with.
