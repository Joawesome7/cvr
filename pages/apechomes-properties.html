Apec Homes Facts

Key information about Apec Homes' affordability and market focus.

🏠

Mass Housing Leader

For over two decades, Apec Homes has been a dominant force in Philippine mass housing, successfully delivering tens of thousands of homes to minimum and median wage earners.

💰

Highly Attainable

They specialize in properties specifically priced to maximize Pag-IBIG housing loans, ensuring monthly amortizations remain well within the budget of the average Filipino worker.

🗺️

Transit-Oriented

Despite their affordability, Apec Homes developments are strategically placed near provincial transport terminals and main highways to ensure residents can easily commute to Metro Manila.
